* samplers
 - DEMC is implemented but not well exercised
  - Required local patch of PyMC bug
  - Implemented hybrid of DEMetropolisZ and DEMetropolis to take advantage of exploration benefits of intra-chain exchange (DEMetropolis) while maintaining independence for proper convergence stats (DEMetropolisZ uses its own history)
   - This is better than EXOFASTv2's sampler, which is equivalent to DEMetropolis/setting n_groups=1
 - Implemented Parallel tempering + Differential Evolution sampler, analogous to EXOFASTv2's sampler
  - Better than HMC for multi-modal exploration (like we expect for mulensing)
  - HMC doesn't play well with gradient free Op implementation of MulensModel
  - adaptive step sizes to optimize temperature scaling
  - Robbins-Monro temperature ladder adaptation (Vousden, Farr & Mandel 2016,
    ptemcee): after each swap attempt between rungs k and k+1, update
    Δ(log T_k) += γ_n · (accepted - r*), γ_n ~ 1/n^0.7, r* ≈ 0.234;
    freeze ladder at end of tune phase. Eliminates need to hand-tune T_max.
    ptde.py already has adapt_ladder / target_swap_rate stubs for this.
  - check that we're actually using numpyro when specified
