* vcve parameterization:
 - refactor to remove the sign parameter. that means computing both solutions and incorporating the joint logaddexp summed likelihood, with soft and hard shields that allow us to compute a single log likelihood when given an imaginary/non-physical e. The soft shield (sigmoid) guides the sampler and protects the physics. the hard shield (clip) is a stop to protect the math.
 - cosw/sinw must be gaussian normals mu=0, sigma=1 (no bias to omega, no weird boundaries).
